Why You Should List With Us?

Ekaralu is more than just a platform for buying and selling farmland in Telangana. We are a dedicated team of
professionals committed to making the process as easy and efficient as possible for you.


Direct Communication

With Ekaralu, you communicate directly with potential
buyers. This means you have complete control over who
sees your property and when. It saves you time and
ensures that your property is seen by serious buyers.

Comprehensive Information

We provide detailed information about each property,
which allows buyers to learn a lot about your property
without having to visit it in person. This saves time for
both you and the buyer and helps make the decision
process quicker and more efficient.

Strong Network

Over the years, we've built a strong network of investors,
consultants, landlords, and influencers. This network
helps us deliver a smooth buying and selling experience
to our clients.

Informed Decisions

We have analyzed over 10,000 properties using 33+
parameters specific to agricultural farmlands in
Telangana. This detailed analysis helps us and our
clients make informed decisions, ensuring you get the
best value for your investment.

Easy Listing Process

Listing your property with us is simple and quick. If you
need any help at any stage, our customer service team
is always ready to assist you.

Genuine Enquiries:

Once your listing is live, you will start receiving enquiries
from genuine buyers. We ensure that every enquiry is
verified to avoid any inconvenience.


If you have a property to list, you can email us at hello@ekaralu.in or click on the 'Post Your
Property' button on our website. We're here to make the process of selling your farmland
as easy as possible. Choose Ekaralu, where selling farmland is made simple, informed, and
efficient!
