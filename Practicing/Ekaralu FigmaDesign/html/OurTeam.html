EkaraluTeam

Ekaralu is the brainchild of a team of dedicated professionals who are passionate about making the process of buying and
selling agricultural farmland in Telangana as easy and efficient as possible. Here's a little about our team:


Market Research Team

Our research team is adept at gathering information about agricultural farmlands from various sources, including the internet, local networks, and
print media. The team uses data science to analyze the collected data, helping us identify potential farmlands to invest in and recommend to our
clients.

Field Team

Our field team is meticulous in its search for, inspection of, and evaluation of farmlands. They conduct in-depth due diligence on properties,
perform local physical inspections, build local contacts, and provide our clients with high-potential agricultural farmlands.

Legal Team

Our legal team, consisting of experienced lawyers, conducts thorough research into the legal aspects of property titles before agreeing to any
transactions. This ensures that our clients' legal dealings are reliable and stress-free.

Operations Team

Our operations team, made up of project managers and supervisors, is well-versed in implementing best practices. With extensive knowledge in
agricultural operations, local networking, technology, equipment, and labor sourcing, the team ensures effective project management and
resource utilization.

Farmers Team

Our farmers team is a group of experienced farmers from various locations throughout our farmlands. They work together to manage the growth
of lawns, sapling plantations, and crops, among other things, to meet the needs of the customer. Their on-the-ground knowledge adds value to
the land.

Our vision is to make the process of buying and selling agricultural farmland a hassle-free experience. We believe in the power of
information symmetry and efficiency, and we strive to eliminate the challenges faced by buyers and sellers in the agricultural farmland
market. Choose Ekaralu, where finding the perfect farmland is made simple, informed, and efficient!
